Debrisoft

Debrisoft

In the initial stages, when any bleeding has stopped, any necrotic tissue, slough, exudate and foreign bodies will need removing from the wound. Debrisoft offers an immediate and gentle debriding option.

Find out more about Debrisoft

The Wound Progression Model

The appearance and characteristics of a wound will change as it progresses through the healing process and therefore the management of that wound may need to change too. Our advanced wound dressings aim to support the wound and optimise healing at each dierent stage.

The Wound Progression Model has been reproduced with kind permission from Georgie Hollis at The Veterinary Wound Library.

Suprasorb A+AG

Suprasorb A+AG

SuprasorbA+Ag is an aliginate dressing which turns into a gel on contact with exudate. Contains silver ions for critically colonised or at risk wounds.
