Debrisoft®

Rapid, effective yet gentle debridement

18 million specially angled fibres sweep and bind slough, debris and non-viable tissue from the wound bed leaving viable granulation tissue and epithelial cells intact

Cost-efficient debridement without the need for surgery

In under 10 minutes Debrisoft facilitates wound assessment, reduces the risk of infection and allows the healing process to commence. It can be used by nurses who do not sharp debride.

Debrisoft can be used post-surgical debridement and as an alternative to wet-to-dry or honey dressings.

Equine Case study

Debrisoft® was used for 8 minutes to debride a 3 month old wound on the front of the hock.
